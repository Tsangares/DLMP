Privacy Policy
How we protect and handle your information at YQue.net
Last Updated: August 1st 2024

Overview

yque.net is a privacy-focused service that allows users to create digital profiles for physical devices through NFC tags and QR codes. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Email address (optional, only when contacting support)
  • Phone Numbers: Only when you specifically request custom notifications that you design
  • Profile Images: Single avatar image per profile (processed to remove metadata and crop as needed)
  • Profile Content: Information you choose to attach to your physical devices

Information We Collect Automatically

  • IP Addresses: Collected solely for throttling and spam prevention purposes
  • User Agent Information: Browser and device information for throttling and spam prevention
  • Session Data: Minimal session identifiers may be stored via cookies to maintain login sessions

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Operating and maintaining the yque.net service
  • Preventing spam and abuse through IP-based throttling
  • Providing customer support when you contact us
  • Delivering custom notifications you specifically request and design
  • Maintaining your device profiles and associated content

Data Processing and Security

Image Processing

  • All uploaded images have their metadata automatically stripped for privacy
  • Images are cropped as needed for the service
  • You maintain full control to delete your images at any time

Data Security

  • Our database is hosted on a secure server
  • We implement privacy-focused practices throughout our infrastructure
  • No user accounts require traditional login credentials or stored passwords

Data Retention

  • IP Addresses and User Agent Data: Retained for 1 year for spam prevention and security purposes
  • Profile Images and Content: Stored until you choose to delete them
  • Contact Information: Retained only as long as necessary to provide requested services

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics services or tracking technologies.

No Index Policy: Our service does not maintain a searchable index of user profiles or content.

Third-Party Services

yque.net does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We operate independently to maintain your privacy.

Account Access

  • Accounts must be purchased through authorized retail locations
  • No traditional user registration or password-based login system
  • Access requirements are designed to ensure service quality and security

Your Rights and Controls

You have the right to:

  • Delete your uploaded images at any time
  • Contact us regarding your data through our support form
  • Request information about data we may have collected about you

Age Requirements

There are no specific age restrictions for using yque.net, though account acquisition requires purchase through retail channels with standard payment method requirements.

Geographic Scope

yque.net is available globally with no geographic restrictions. We are committed to privacy regardless of your location.

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies solely for maintaining login sessions. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of yque.net after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us through the support form available on yque.net.

This privacy policy reflects our commitment to privacy-first design and minimal data collection practices.
