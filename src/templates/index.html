Deep Link Micro Pages
A small web page that travels with a physical thing.

Every DLMP product has a QR code printed on it. Scan the code and it opens a small web page tied to that one item. Whoever owns the item owns the page. You can put links, text, and a picture on it, and change them whenever you want.

Each product also comes with a passcode. Enter the passcode on your page and you can edit it. There is no sign-up and no email needed. To open a page, scan its QR code or enter its account ID below.
